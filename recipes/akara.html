Pap and Akara

Pap and Akara with the saturday vibes

This is a food that is mostly eaten in Nigeria saturday. Very light and yet very nutritious food

Ingredients

Akara;
  • Beans
  • Fresh pepper
  • Onions
  • salt (to tatse)
  • Water
Pap
  • Ogi (corn extract)
  • Water
  • Honey
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Soak and wash peel off beans when soft, put in a blender with onions and pepper and blend till you get an almost smooth paste
  2. When done, pour into a bowl, add chopped onions and little amount of chopped fresh pepper and add salt to desired taste and stir very well
  3. Pour vegetable oil in a pan and put on medium heat, when hot reduce to low heat and scoop spoon sized balls of the beans paste into the oil then deep fry both sides till golden brown colored Akarais gotten then remove and keep in a container.
  4. For the pap, boil water, then put ogi in a plate in desired amount and mix with small water till it forms a paste, when the water boils 100Â° pour the water into your mixture till it is thicken and add honey and milk.
